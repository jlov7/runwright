# Frontend Copy Tone Guidelines

## Tone goals
- Confident and actionable.
- Specific about next actions.
- Calm under failure states.

## Rules
- Start with outcome, then instruction.
- Use plain language for errors and recovery.
- Keep success copy concise and progress-oriented.
- Avoid internal jargon unless mirrored in commands/endpoints.

## Applied examples
- Success: "Profile created: <handle>"
- Recovery: "Publish failed and was rolled back: <reason>"
- Guidance: "Create a profile first." / "Retry queue cleared."

# Legal And Compliance Pack

## Required Documents
- Terms of Service.
- Privacy Policy.
- UGC/Creator policy and moderation standards.
- Telemetry and diagnostics consent language.

## Compliance Controls
- Data minimization for telemetry payloads.
- Secret redaction in crash envelopes.
- Documented retention windows for operational data.
- Auditability of release artifacts and provenance evidence.

## Review Gates
1. Legal review signoff.
2. Security review signoff.
3. Product policy review signoff.
4. Final release manager approval.
